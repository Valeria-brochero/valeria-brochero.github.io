To-Be System Model

Business and Systems Analysis

To-be system models are meant to show your proposed future process. This model was designed while considering the implications of a new scheduling system to be implemented for Kid's Center for Pediatric Therapies, a non-profit organization in need of a more effecient website. My team and I researched what the new software would be capable of, and I had the pleasure of designing the new flow of information.

Business Process Analysis

Business and Systems Analysis

In this excel table my teammate and I identify and benchmark business processes that are supported by the web sites of similar organizations to the Kid Center's. We determined “best practice” for the client through analysis of how the website may be used to support business processes and activities. Together, we reviewed the web sites of similar organizations with the same general domain and mission. The idea was to focus on analysis of the business process/activity, rather than on how well it is implemented on the web site.

Gantt Chart

Business and Systems Analysis

The Gantt Chart is meant to be used as a tool to help track progress as we move throughout different phases of a project. It serves as a visual representation of what is finished, what needs to be completed, and what tasks are dependent on each other. It also details who has been assigned to what task, which helps keep us all accountable for our individual portions.
