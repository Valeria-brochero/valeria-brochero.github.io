Gantt Chart

Business and Systems Analysis

The Gantt Chart is meant to be used as a tool to help track progress as we move throughout different phases of a project. It serves as a visual representation of what is finished, what needs to be completed, and what tasks are dependent on each other. It also details who has been assigned to what task, which helps keep everyone accountable for their individual portions to ensure the project may be delivered on time.
